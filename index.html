About me

Hi I am Paul, an aspiring software developer.

I have been coding for the better part of 6 years now, starting in college. I have always been fascinated by video games and have dreamed of making them. However my coding journey has veered to the more practical as I started learning the basics. An intro to Java class in college was my first real exposure to programming, but as it was not my major did not go further. I set programming down for about a year and then started to watch YouTube tutorials on Python, and more specifically the PyGame library. I spent a few years making small games and programs with them before starting my biggest project, an engine using PyGame that would streamline the process. I used knowledge I had gained from those YouTube tutorials as well as from devlogs posted by people making games using more common tools like Unity, Godot, and Unreal. It currently stands as a functional library but I still want to make changes and improvements.

As I advanced further with my skills I wanted to find more formal instruction, and found Codecademy. For the last year and change I have been working through their courses, learning new skills and trying to grow as a developer. I decided I would take the full stack web development course as a new marketable skill to learn. I have been working through it slowly but surely over the last year. I have taken a few detours to take courses on other topics that interest me, such as advanced Python, Flask, Java, C++, and more. I always am interested in learning more and expanding my skills so that I can figure out new ways to solve problems.
